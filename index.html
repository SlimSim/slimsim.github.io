Petra - Personal trainer!

This app will help you with your workouts! Simply start a workout and Petra will tell you what exercise to do and for how long. This app will coach you through good workouts!

Simply start a workout, turn up the volume and Petra will tell you what exercise to do and for how long via the speakers.

Petra has a built in timer and comes with a few pre-programmed workouts, including the popular "7 Minute Workout", a core workout and a leg workout. More workouts will be added as time goes by.

You can also create your own workouts with your own exercises, simply give your workout a name and write a comma or dot separated list of exercises at the bottom and your good to go!

Petra will also tell you the half time, allowing you to switch from one side to another if you need.

Petra has five types of workouts, tabata workout, double workout, single workout, Serious stretch and quick stretch.

The double workout is the longest and most intense program:

  1. First exercise for 45 seconds
  2. Second exercise for 45 seconds
  3. The first exercise again for 30 seconds
  4. The second exercise again for 30 seconds
  5. Then a pause for 15 seconds

Then the next set starts with the third and fourth exercises...

The single workout is slightly shorter:

  1. First exercise for 30 seconds
  2. Pause for 10 seconds

Then the next exercise...

The stretching program is the fastest.

  1. First stretching exercise for 20 seconds
Then the next stretch exercise without any pause.

So: try out this app and let Petra take control over your fitness!

Troff - Training with music

Training with music - A Music player that let you loop a part of a song, slow it down and create markers on the song timeline. Troff is a music player that you should use if you need to practice something to music; if you are a dancer working on a choreography or a musician learning to play a song.

Troff helps you with this by adding markers to the song timeline, making it easy to find the parts of the song you need to work on!

Adding a marker before and after a certain part of a song let you loop that part of the song automatically. This way you get the maximum amount of practice time.

Troff lets you easily change the tempo of the song, to let you work on harder parts slowly before you are ready for 100% or even faster!

Need to learn a routine from a movie? No problem; Troff also supports movies! You can step in the movie frame by frame for more detail. You can still use markers, change tempo and loop sequences with the film clip.

Troff also lets you zoom in the timeline, color the markers, and write longer text for memory to each marker.

Follow Troff on facebook.com/troffmusic to get the latest information!
